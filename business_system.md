# Gym and Vending Café Business System

## Business Concept
This business combines a vending café and a fitness gym in one two-floor building. The first floor offers quick food and drinks, while the second floor provides gym facilities for exercise and wellness.

## Business Layout
### First Floor: Vending Café
- Vending machines for snacks, meals, bottled drinks, and sports drinks
- Tables and chairs for customers to sit, relax, and eat
- Convenient for gym members, students, workers, and walk-in customers

### Second Floor: Gym Area
- Treadmills, weights, and exercise machines
- Facilities for general fitness, strength training, and weight loss
- Allows customers to work out and refresh in one location

## Owner Responsibilities
- Handle maintenance and operations support
- Check and maintain gym equipment
- Monitor vending machines
- Keep the building clean and organized
- Handle minor repairs and safety checks
- Coordinate restocking and servicing when needed

## Operations System
### Daily
- Open the building and inspect cleanliness, lighting, and safety
- Check vending machines and restock items if needed
- Inspect equipment for damage or malfunction
- Maintain a welcoming environment for customers

### Weekly
- Review machine performance and stock levels
- Tighten or repair minor issues on gym equipment
- Clean high-use areas thoroughly

### Monthly
- Perform deeper maintenance and safety audits
- Review customer experience and identify improvement areas

## Target Customers
- Gym members
- Students
- Office workers
- Health-conscious individuals
- People looking for quick snacks and drinks

## Business Purpose
The purpose of this business is to provide a convenient place where people can focus on health, fitness, and relaxation. By combining a gym and vending café, the business gives customers comfort, convenience, and value.
