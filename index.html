How membership works

Membership is meant to stay simple: choose a pass, check staff availability, then request the coach or barista you prefer. Coach sessions are scheduled by time slot so the gym floor stays manageable. Cafe orders can be prepared by the barista on duty when your visit starts, during your workout, or after your cool-down.

Good to know

Trust, safety, and visit policies

Can I choose my coach or barista?

Yes, you can request one. The final assignment depends on the selected day, time, and current bookings.

How are coach sessions handled?

Coach sessions are scheduled by time slot so equipment use stays organized and every customer gets enough attention.

What if my schedule changes?

Send a message before your visit time. Staff can move your request to the next available slot.

How is the space maintained?

High-touch equipment is checked daily, vending stock is reviewed, and cleaning is handled before opening and during shift changes.
